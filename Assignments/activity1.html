Learning about colors

The purpose of this is to practice HTML and outline primary and secondary colors.

Primary Colors

Primary colors are colors that can be combined to form other colors. They are:

  • Red
  • Blue
  • Yellow

Secondary Colors

Secondary colors are colors made from combining the primary colors. They are:

  • Purple
  • Orange
  • Green

Summary

Primary colors (red, blue, yellow) are the colors that cannot be made by mixing other colors, but can be mixed themselves to form secondary colors (purple, orange, green).
